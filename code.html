An online platform that provides educational programs, training, or learning resources through the internet is known as an e-learning website, or electronic learning website. E-learning platforms provide students a flexible and convenient approach to pick up knowledge and skills on a variety of topics. These websites are meant to support self-paced learning, frequently by combining text-based material with multimedia, interactive features, and evaluations. On these platforms, learners frequently need to set up user accounts or profiles. Various course materials, such as video lectures, textual materials, tests, assignments, and interactive simulations, are hosted by e-learning websites. They can access course materials and keep track of their progress after registering. E-learning websites are convenient for students no matter where they are because they can be accessed from any location with an internet connection. To improve the learning experience, several e-learning platforms provide interactive features including discussion boards, chat rooms, and virtual labs. Students can keep track of their progress through the course, examine certificates of completion, and occasionally earn badges or certifications upon passing it.Quizzes, examinations, and assignments are frequently included in e-learning platforms to assess a learner's comprehension of the subject matter. Assessment feedback aids in learning improvement.
